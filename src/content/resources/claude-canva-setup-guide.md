## What's Inside

This free 3-page guide walks you through connecting Claude to Canva and generating on-brand designs without ever leaving your Claude conversation.

**You'll learn:**
- The exact steps to **connect Claude to Canva** in Settings → Integrations (2-minute setup)
- How to configure your **Canva Brand Kit** so designs automatically use your fonts, colors, and logo
- **Copy-paste prompts** for generating slides, Instagram posts, carousels, and quote graphics
- A **prompt tips table** showing what to add to every request for better, faster results
- The **quick-start checklist** to go from setup to published content in under 30 minutes

## Who This Is For

This is for content creators, solopreneurs, and marketers who spend too much time bouncing between writing tools and design tools. If you've ever pasted text into Canva and spent 20 minutes fixing fonts and brand colors, this integration eliminates that entirely.

No design experience needed. No coding required. Just a Claude account and a Canva account.
