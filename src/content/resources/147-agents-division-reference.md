## What's Inside

agency-agents is organized like a real agency — 12 divisions, each staffed with specialists who have defined roles, proven workflows, and specific deliverables. This reference guide maps the entire roster so you always know which division handles what.

The repo hit 77,000 GitHub stars because people realized the same thing: **generic AI is a generalist intern**. The agency-agents roster gives you department heads — a Security Engineer who enforces threat modeling, a Brand Guardian who pushes back on off-brand copy, a Growth Hacker who thinks in acquisition loops, not vague "growth tips."

**What's covered:**
- **Engineering Division** (26 agents) — Frontend, backend, mobile, AI, DevOps, security, SRE, code review, database, architecture, incident response
- **Marketing Division** (28 agents) — TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, SEO, Reddit, social strategy, AI citation optimization
- **Design Division** (8 agents) — UI/UX, brand identity, Whimsy Injector, visual storytelling, image prompting, inclusive visuals
- **Paid Media Division** (7 agents) — PPC, ad creative, paid social, tracking, programmatic, full audits
- **Sales + Product Divisions** — Outbound, deal strategy, sales coaching, proposals, product management, sprint planning, behavioral nudge design

## Who This Is For

Anyone who wants a birds-eye view of the full agency-agents roster before deciding which agents to activate. Use this as a reference when starting a new project — figure out which division you need, then activate the right specialist.
