## What's Inside

This checklist covers everything Apple's human reviewer looks for when they pick up your app for the first time. Most App Store rejections come from the same handful of fixable issues — placeholder content, missing demo credentials, IAP violations, and permission strings that don't justify themselves.

**The checklist is organized into 7 categories:**

- **Content & UI** — Buttons, placeholders, and screenshot accuracy
- **Login & Access** — Demo credentials, Sign in with Apple requirements
- **Payments** — IAP-only rules, no Stripe, no external buy links
- **Permissions** — Justification strings, no pre-emptive requests
- **Account Management** — In-app deletion (mandatory since 2022)
- **AI Disclosure** — Apple's newest requirement, most builders miss it
- **Before You Submit** — Final checks before hitting the button

## Who This Is For

You built something with AI — vibe-coded an app, shipped a side project, or used Claude/GPT to accelerate development. You're about to submit to the App Store for the first time (or the second time, after getting rejected the first time). This checklist takes 10 minutes to run through and saves weeks of rejection cycles.
