## What's Inside

This guide covers the complete setup to get Claude Code responding in iMessage on your iPhone — straight from your Mac, no API key, no extra app.

**You'll get:**
- **Bun install** — every channel plugin requires Bun; without it nothing runs (10-second install)
- **The one command** that starts the iMessage channel (and why running it wrong causes the `--channels ignored` error)
- **Full Disk Access setup** — macOS blocks the plugin without it, and it's a 30-second toggle most people miss
- **Self-chat test** — verify it's working before you tell anyone
- **Access control commands** — keep Claude to yourself or open it up to specific contacts with a single command

## Who This Is For

If you use Claude Code and want to reach it from your iPhone without opening your laptop, this is the setup. It works over your existing Apple ID — no new accounts, no subscriptions. Everything runs locally on your Mac.

Takes about 2 minutes if you follow the steps in order.
