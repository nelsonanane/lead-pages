## What's Inside

You've heard that specialized AI agents outperform generic ones. The evidence is clear: when you give Claude a specific identity, workflow, and set of deliverables to optimize for, the output quality jumps dramatically. The problem is knowing **which agent to reach for** when you have 147 options.

This cheatsheet solves that. It's organized by what you're actually trying to do — not by what the agent is called.

**Need to build something?** There's a section for that. **Running ads?** Section for that. **Creating content?** Growing a business? Writing code? Every common task maps to the right specialist — immediately.

**What's in the cheatsheet:**
- **When You're Building Something** — Frontend, backend, mobile, security, code review, database, DevOps
- **When You're Creating Content** — TikTok, LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, SEO, podcasting
- **When You're Running Ads** — Google Ads, Meta, tracking, creative, full audits
- **When You're Growing a Business** — Acquisition, prospecting, product, research, feedback
- **Power Combos** — Multi-agent workflows for launches, campaigns, new features, and content batches

## Who This Is For

Anyone who installed agency-agents and felt overwhelmed by 147 options — or anyone who wants to get the most out of their AI tools by using the right specialist for the right job. This cheatsheet is the reference card you keep open while you work.
