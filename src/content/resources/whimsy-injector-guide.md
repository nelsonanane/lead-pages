## What's Inside

The Whimsy Injector is the most underestimated agent in the agency-agents repo. Everyone focuses on the technical ones — Frontend Developer, Security Engineer, PPC Strategist. The Whimsy Injector sounds like a joke.

It isn't. It's the agent that makes the difference between **content people share** and **content people scroll past**.

Generic AI optimizes for "correct." The Whimsy Injector optimizes for "memorable." It has a framework for WHERE personality belongs, HOW MUCH to add, and what TYPE — an unexpected analogy, a self-aware aside, a micro-moment of delight. Not "make this funnier." That prompt gets you jokes nobody asked for.

**What's in the guide:**
- **What the Whimsy Injector actually does** — and why it's not just "add humor"
- **Setup in 2 steps** — install agency-agents, activate the agent
- **Before vs. after examples** — email subject lines, landing page headers, social CTAs
- **The Rule of One** — one injection per section; more tips it into performance
- **When NOT to use it** — legal copy, crisis comms, high-stakes technical docs
- **4 pieces of content to run it on first** — where personality pays off the most

## Who This Is For

Anyone whose AI-generated content gets read but not remembered, shared, or acted on. If your copy sounds like it was written by committee — technically fine, emotionally flat — this is the agent and the guide that fixes it.
