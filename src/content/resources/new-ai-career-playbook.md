## What's Inside

This playbook gives you the exact path from "I've heard these new AI roles exist" to "I have a positioning, a portfolio piece, and active conversations" — in 30 days.

**You'll get:**
- All 5 new AI job categories defined with pay rates and real hiring context
- How to pick the right role based on your existing skills and background
- The one portfolio piece that opens doors for each role — what it is and how to build it fast
- The LinkedIn strategy: why searching for these titles returns almost no results right now, and how that's your advantage
- 30-day action plan with specific steps by week

## Who This Is For

People who are technically curious but not developers. People who have domain expertise (law, medicine, finance, HR, operations) and want to apply it to AI work. People who feel like AI is accelerating and they haven't found their entry point yet.

This is also for people who are worried about their current role. The reframe: AI is creating new roles faster than it's eliminating existing ones in knowledge work. The catch is most people don't know the new job titles. This playbook fixes that.

## The Window

These roles are new enough that most hiring managers are still figuring out what they need. A candidate who walks in with a clear framework ("I specialize in LLM quality control for legal workflows") will shape the role in their own terms. That advantage shrinks as the market matures. This is the early window.
