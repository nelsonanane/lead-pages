This guide adapts the Claude-Code-Game-Studios model (10,000+ GitHub stars in one day) for creative service businesses — writing agencies, design studios, marketing operations, development shops.

**You'll get:**
- The 5-layer agent hierarchy: Director, Specialist, Review, Editor, and You

- Realistic pricing for a one-person AI agency (what to charge, what not to say)

## Who This Is For

Freelancers, solopreneurs, and creative service providers who want to increase their output capacity without hiring a full team. If you're currently turning down work because you don't have enough hours — this is the model to study.

This isn't for people who want to disappear from the work. The agents execute. You still direct, review, and deliver. The leverage is real — but it's leverage, not replacement.

## The Core Insight

One person with a 49-agent AI team is competing with agencies charging 10x more for the same output quality. The differentiator isn't technology — it's whether you know what excellent looks like. The people who succeed with this model are the ones who have strong taste and can recognize when output meets the bar. That's the skill that compounds.
